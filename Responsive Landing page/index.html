The Core Problem: The "Expat Parcel Paradox

The rise of global mobility has created a new challenge: people living abroad often struggle to send and receive goods from their home countries. Traditional international shipping is notorious for being: Expensive and Opaque: Hidden customs fees, duties, and brokerage charges can make a simple package cost a small fortune. Slow and Unreliable: Packages are often delayed in customs and can take weeks or even months to arrive. Complex: The paperwork and regulations for each country are a nightmare to navigate. Globeship is designed to solve this "Expat Parcel Paradox" by making international shipping as simple, transparent, and affordable as domestic shipping.
